How it works

Connect a Telegram bot, configure what matters, then review prioritized events.

1

Connect Telegram

Add a Telegram bot token to a monitoring profile. The service connects to this bot only, not to your personal Telegram account.

2

Configure signals

Choose what to track: leads, complaints, requests, urgent messages or general activity. Optionally set profile-level AI limits.

3

Review events

InboxSignal shows structured events instead of raw message noise. Urgent events can trigger Telegram alerts.

Start with one Telegram monitoring profile.

Keep the MVP simple: one bot, one profile, clear rules, selective AI usage and predictable alerting.
