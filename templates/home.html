What the system does

It is not a chat client. It is a filtering and prioritization layer for Telegram communication.

🎯

Detects important signals

Finds leads, complaints, booking requests and urgent messages without forcing you to read every incoming message manually.

🧠

Enriches events with AI

Adds category, priority score, short summary and extracted data like contact, budget, product or requested time.

🚨

Sends actionable alerts

Creates alerts for urgent or important events and avoids noisy duplicate notifications through cooldown and deduplication.
